Privacy Protocol: Your Data is Yours

TV Stats is built for the Silent Observer. All of your movie and TV history—including watchlists and ratings—is stored locally on your device in JSON format. We do not own, manage, or have access to your personal database.

We do not use tracking cookies and collect zero personal information. Because your data lives on your hardware, we cannot recover it if deleted. We recommend keeping a backup of your JSON files if you wish to save your history long-term.

MacOS Installation

Since TV Stats is downloaded directly, macOS may flag it as being from an "unidentified developer." This is a standard security gate that you can bypass in seconds:

  • Locate the app in your Downloads folder.
  • Right-Click (or Control-Click) the icon and select Open.
  • When the dialog appears, click Open again. You only need to do this once.
  • If "Open" doesn't appear, go to System Settings > Privacy & Security and click Open Anyway.
