Privacy Protocol: Your Data is Yours

TV Stats is built for the Silent Observer. All of your movie and TV history is stored locally on your device in JSON format. We do not own, manage, or have access to your personal database.

We collect zero personal information. Because your data lives on your hardware, we cannot recover it if deleted. We recommend keeping a backup of your JSON files.
