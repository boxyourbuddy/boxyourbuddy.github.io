Privacy Protocol: Your Data is Yours

TV Stats is built for the Silent Observer. Every piece of watch history—ratings, dates, and rewatch counts—is stored locally on your Mac in a standard JSON format.

We believe in forensic privacy. We don't use tracking cookies, we don't have a cloud database, and we have zero access to your viewing habits. Your history is your business.

MacOS Installation Instructions

Because TV Stats is independent software, macOS may flag it as an "unverified developer." This is a security feature, not a bug.

To install: Right-Click the app icon, select Open, and then click Open again in the security window. You only have to do this once to grant permission.
